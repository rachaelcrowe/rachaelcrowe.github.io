About Me

Hello, my name is Rachael Crowe. I live in Orlando, Florida. I have loved anything art since I was young. At age six, I started taking one-on-one art lessons until I was old enough to experiment with art myself. I focus on pencil drawing and watercolor painting mostly. I created this website to keep up with this passion of mine, and so that I can pursue it as a career. I am currently a sophomore at Valencia College, working towards my Associates of Arts degree. I plan to move on to a university where I can study graphic design and studio art.
